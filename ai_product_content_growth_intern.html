Go-To-Market Internship ⚲ Hangzhou / Remote

Content & User Growth Intern

Job Description

You will work on content and growth for Vattention's overseas creator audience, turning AI video product features, use cases, and user stories into content and growth experiments across TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, X, Discord, Reddit, and related channels.

Responsibilities:

  • Plan and operate overseas social content, responding quickly to AI product trends, competitor moves, and global internet culture with short-form video and social ideas.
  • Create product tutorials, use-case demos, user stories, and creator challenges that help creators understand the product's value faster.
  • Build and maintain a database of overseas creators, KOLs, and KOCs, supporting partnership outreach, content co-creation, and seed-user relationships.
  • Help operate overseas communities such as Discord, Reddit, and Telegram, collecting user feedback and turning it into product and content iteration.
  • Support acquisition, activation, referral, and UGC campaigns, monitor data performance, and explore low-cost acquisition channels and high-potential user segments.

Qualifications:

  • Overseas living, studying, working, or deep community experience; familiar with global culture, social media ecosystems, and user behavior; able to work in English.
  • Currently pursuing a bachelor's degree or above; media, marketing, advertising, film production, design, product, or related majors are preferred.
  • Heavy user of TikTok, Reels, Shorts, X, or similar platforms, with strong sensitivity to global trends, meme culture, and short-form video pacing.
  • Familiar with at least one AI video or editing tool, such as CapCut, Runway, Pika, Sora, Premiere, or Final Cut Pro.
  • Basic data literacy, able to read social platform analytics, Google Analytics, or campaign data and adjust content and growth actions accordingly.
  • Self-driven, outcome-oriented, fast to test and iterate, and willing to keep learning and shipping in a fast-changing AI industry.

Nice to Have:

  • You operate your own overseas-facing social account; follower count is not required, but we would love to see your content style and reflection.
  • Personal short-form video portfolio, creator collaboration experience, cross-cultural teamwork, or prior overseas community growth projects.

Working Details:

  • Location: Hangzhou / remote, with cross-time-zone collaboration.
  • Start date: ASAP.
  • Duration: at least 3 months, 4+ days per week.
