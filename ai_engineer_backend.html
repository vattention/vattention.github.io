AI Engineer, Backend & Agent

Job Description

As a software engineer of backend and agent, you'll be responsible for building platforms that support our product features. Sometimes this means inventing highly available distributed systems. Sometimes this means handling the challenges of scaling to millions of creators.

Responsibilities:

  • Design and develop backend architecture for AI core products, building highly available and low-latency backend systems
  • Develop video AI Agent features including decision-making, scheduling, tool module implementation, and multimodal data processing (text/image/audio/video)
  • Develop cloud platform services, infrastructure and architecture setup and optimization, build comprehensive CI/CD pipelines based on Docker, K8s, structured logging, distributed tracing and performance monitoring systems
  • Work closely with product, client and algorithm teams to clarify technical requirements and implementation paths, jointly advance AI products from concept to implementation
  • Drive project advancement and core team building with strong ownership

Requirements:

  • Bachelor's degree or above in Computer Science, Information Technology, Software Engineering or related fields, solid computer foundation
  • 3+ years of backend development experience, 1+ year of AI-related experience
  • Familiar with Python/FastAPI and other common frameworks, solid backend development and distributed systems experience, proficient in MySQL/PostgreSQL/Redis/Kafka and other common infrastructure services
  • Experience in AI technology engineering implementation, familiar with AI Agent, Workflow, RAG and other core concepts, familiar with pgvector, Pinecone and other vector databases
  • Cloud-native and engineering practice capabilities: familiar with at least one mainstream cloud platform (AWS, Alibaba Cloud, etc.), cloud service deployment experience; familiar with Docker, K8s and other container technologies, master CI/CD construction, log monitoring and troubleshooting methods
  • Familiar with video development processes and AI applications in video scenarios (such as intelligent editing, content generation, subtitle generation, etc.) preferred
  • Open mindset and technical problem-solving ability, willing to actively learn new technologies, able to quickly adapt to business changes and solve complex technical challenges
  • Self-driven with stronger initiative than 80% of peers; highly hands-on and practical
  • Enjoy helping and supporting team members
  • Strong curiosity to explore new technologies and experiment with new products, coupled with a willingness to share insights
