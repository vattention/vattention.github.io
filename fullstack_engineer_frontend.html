Engineering Full-time ⚲ Hangzhou / Beijing

Full Stack Engineer, Frontend Focus

Job Description

Build professional-grade video post-production full-stack applications based on Electron + React + Python/Node, covering the complete workflow from frontend rendering to local data storage.

Responsibilities:

  • Full-stack Development: Build professional-grade video post-production full-stack applications based on Electron + React + Python/Node, covering the complete workflow from frontend rendering to local data storage
  • Performance Optimization: Design high-performance rendering architecture to ensure extreme smoothness in complex timeline, multi-track editing, and real-time preview scenarios
  • Architecture & Services: Establish engineering standards, optimize IPC process communication; independently responsible for Python/Node backend services (such as FastAPI) development, business logic processing, and data synchronization

Qualifications:

  • Frontend Core: Proficient in React / TypeScript, with the ability to build complex interactive interfaces and highly maintainable component architectures
  • Electron Expert: Have production-level project experience, proficient in main/renderer process communication, Node native capabilities, system calls, and packaging/updating processes
  • Backend Capabilities: Master Python/Node and common infrastructure (SQL/NoSQL, MQ, Redis), with solid API design, security, and performance optimization capabilities

Bonus Points:

  • Low-level Acceleration: Familiar with WebAssembly/WebCodecs/ffmpeg/H.264+ etc.
  • AI: Experience in editing engine, AI Agent/LLM/RAG development, or on-device inference optimization
  • Community/Creation: Video creation experience, open source projects/technical blogs, etc.

You might be a fit if you ...

  • Extremely strong curiosity and self-drive (Top 20%), highly hands-on, enjoy sharing
  • Willing to trust teammates first, comfortable expressing genuine ideas, without overthinking
  • Find joy in helping and supporting teammates

About Vattention

Vattention is a company dedicated to the transformative power of Human-AI Co-creation, believing it to be fundamental to the future of content creation. We build intelligent productivity tools that empower creators to bring their visions to life with unparalleled efficiency and impact.

Our organization is highly flat, and our team is small and talent dense. We especially appreciate people who seek truth, are passionate, and possess creativity. We thrive on intense debates, wild ideas, and shipping code.
