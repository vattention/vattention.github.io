AI Engineer, Model Algorithm

Job Description

Design and develop model algorithms for AI core products, build data and training processes, create highly available and low-latency model algorithm systems.

Responsibilities:

  • Responsible for the full pipeline of post-training for large video understanding models, including SFT, RM training, RLHF/PPO/DPO optimization, to enhance model understanding in video scenarios.
  • Build aligned data systems for the video domain, focusing on data construction, selection, and quality control to support model performance in core tasks such as video content analysis.
  • Research video temporal modeling and multimodal reasoning technologies to optimize model performance for complex video tasks (e.g., plot decomposition, content summarization).
  • Apply model compression and acceleration techniques to balance video understanding accuracy and inference efficiency, driving technology implementation in products.
  • Drive project advancement and core team building with strong ownership

Qualifications:

  • Master's degree or above in computer science, machine learning or related fields, or bachelor's degree with 3+ years of experience in large model post-training.
  • Proficient in Python and PyTorch; familiar with large model training stacks such as Huggingface, Megatron, and Deepspeed.
  • Practical experience with SFT/RM/RLHF; familiar with DPO/PPO/GRPO and other post-training techniques; experience in video understanding is a plus.
  • Familiar with Transformer architecture and model compression techniques; proficient in efficient parameter tuning methods such as LoRA.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and team spirit; passionate about continuous learning in video AI technologies.
  • Self-driven with stronger initiative than 80% of peers; highly hands-on and practical.
  • Enjoy helping and supporting team members.
  • Strong curiosity to explore new technologies and experiment with new products, coupled with a willingness to share insights.
  • Comfortable expressing genuine ideas openly and avoids overthinking.
