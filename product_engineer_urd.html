Product Engineer, User Research & Data

Job Description

As a Product Engineer of User Research and Data, you will be responsible for bridging user insights and data-driven iteration for next-gen AI creativity tools.

Responsibilities:

  • Management of labeled data for model post-training
  • Product feature breakdown in video post-production
  • Abstraction and definition of video post-production skills
  • Develop tools or scripts for labeling and user research through vibe coding

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree or above in any discipline (STEM, humanities, arts, law, management, etc. are all welcome)
  • Proficient in using AI coding agents such as Cursor, Claude Code, or Augment for simple data processing and rapid prototyping of efficiency-boosting tools
  • Strong command of at least one video post-production tool: Adobe Premiere, Final Cut Pro, DaVinci Resolve, Avid Media Composer, or Vegas Pro
  • Familiar with common video production skills
  • Self-driven with stronger initiative than 80% of peers; highly hands-on and practical
  • Enjoy helping and supporting team members
  • Strong curiosity to explore new technologies and experiment with new products, coupled with a willingness to share insights
  • Comfortable expressing genuine ideas openly and avoids overthinking

About Vattention

Vattention is a company dedicated to the transformative power of Human-AI Co-creation, believing it to be fundamental to the future of content creation. We build intelligent productivity tools that empower creators to bring their visions to life with unparalleled efficiency and impact.

Our organization is highly flat, and our team is small and talent dense. We especially appreciate people who seek truth, are passionate, and possess creativity. We thrive on intense debates, wild ideas, and shipping code.

Our team brings together talent from leading tech companies and top universities, including Alibaba, ByteDance, Tencent, Baidu, Xiaomi, Peking University, Univ. of Sci & Tech of China, Univ. of California Los Angeles, and Sun Yat-sen University . We also have members from AI unicorns such as 01.AI , as well as from content-driven companies and institutions like CapCut, TikTok, Kwai, Joyy, Beijing Film Academy, Wondershare Filmora, and Musical.ly .
