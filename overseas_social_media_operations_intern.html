Go-To-Market Internship ⚲ Hangzhou

Global Social Media Operations Intern

Job Description

You will support Vattention's social media operations for overseas creators and professional users, identifying real discussions, user pain points, and potential collaborators across public communities such as Reddit, Discord, and X, then turning those signals into content topics, interview leads, and operational insights.

Responsibilities:

  • Monitor overseas social media and community channels, collecting high-quality discussion threads, user pain points, topic trends, and potential collaborators.
  • Use real user research data to plan and draft natural English posts, comments, and DMs that encourage professional users to join meaningful discussions.
  • Identify genuine interview prospects from public traffic pools, DMs, and comment sections, assess account quality, organize candidate lists, and draft outreach copy.
  • Produce concise weekly community intelligence reports with post links, topic trends, potential interview targets, and actionable operating suggestions.

Qualifications:

  • Strong English reading and writing skills, with the ability to understand everyday overseas user conversations and write natural, context-aware posts and DMs.
  • Familiar with overseas social and community ecosystems, such as Reddit karma, subreddit rules, and Discord or X community culture; posting or commenting experience is a plus.
  • Detail-oriented, responsible, and fast-learning, able to deliver content drafts, tracking sheets, and user leads on time without missing important interactions.
  • Basic understanding of video editing, creator workflows, or AI video tools, with empathy for creators' practical pain points in content production.

Nice to Have:

  • Internship experience in overseas social media, community operations, or user interviews, with practical familiarity across Reddit, Discord, X / Twitter, or similar platforms.
  • Experience operating your own YouTube, Bilibili, Douyin, or other content account, with an understanding of creators' ideation, production, publishing, and feedback loop.

Working Details:

  • Location: Hangzhou.
  • Start date: ASAP.
  • Duration: at least 3 months preferred, 4+ days per week.
