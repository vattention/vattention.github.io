Growth Engineer

Job Description

You will use engineering to drive growth, turning SEO, long-tail tools, referral and revenue-share systems, KOL campaign tooling, and data analysis into systems that can keep iterating and help Vattention acquire real users at scale.

Responsibilities:

  • Build automated SEO content generation systems covering keyword discovery, page templates, content generation, and landing-page scaling to continuously acquire search traffic.
  • Develop a matrix of lightweight utility pages, such as video downloaders, format converters, online explainers, and related tools, to cover long-tail keywords and capture search traffic.
  • Build referral and revenue-share systems that support invitation flows, configurable payout rules, and campaign analytics for partner promotion and user-led distribution.
  • Develop KOL campaign management tools, including ROI simulation, performance tracking, and automated platform data collection for views, engagement, and conversions.
  • Own website and product analytics instrumentation, user behavior analysis, conversion-rate optimization, and growth experiment evaluation to keep improving growth strategy.

Qualifications:

  • Strong in at least one backend language, such as Python, Node.js, or Go, and able to independently build web features or growth tools.
  • Familiar with modern frontend frameworks such as React or Vue, with the ability to build high-quality interfaces.
  • Strong product sense, able to design technical solutions from the perspective of acquisition, activation, and conversion.
  • Basic data analysis ability and the habit of making decisions based on data.

You might be a fit if you:

  • Want growth engineering to serve marketing, and get energy from building internal tools and shipping quickly.
  • Have experience with programmatic SEO or large-scale landing-page systems.
  • Have built marketing automation, growth tools, or referral systems.
  • Are highly curious and self-driven, strongly hands-on, and willing to share what you learn.
  • Trust teammates, communicate candidly, and avoid unnecessary internal friction.
  • Enjoy helping and supporting team members.
  • Have a strong interest in growth hacking, data-driven decision-making, and marketing automation.
