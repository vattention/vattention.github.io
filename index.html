Human-AI Cocreation

Vattention is a company dedicated to the transformative power of Human-AI Co-creation, believing it to be fundamental to the future of content creation. We build intelligent productivity tools that empower creators to bring their visions to life with unparalleled efficiency and impact.

Our creative agent empowers creators with effortless tool control, minimizing repetitive operations. Blending AI with human creativity, our tools will transcend pure AI, expanding creative frontiers.

We are a dynamic team of researchers and engineers, passionately dedicated to building the cutting-edge software and models that are empowering countless creative professionals behind the scenes. We are thrilled by the impact our work has on those who truly master the art of creation, and we eagerly invite you to join us in shaping the future of creativity.

— Michael, James, and the entire Vattention team
