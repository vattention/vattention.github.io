Job Description

Your mission: Equip millions of creators by reimagining the interaction model for video post-production—making professional editing intuitive.

Responsibilities:

  • Build comprehensive AI-Native interaction design systems for our core products
  • Simplify complexity: Transform advanced video editing capabilities into intuitive interaction experiences
  • Define the future: Explore how AI can reshape creative tools and actively turn that vision into reality

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree or above in fine arts, design, or other artistic disciplines
  • Your portfolio demonstrates a powerful ability to simplify complex problems
  • Be passionate about video creation and excited about the possibilities AI brings
  • Self-driven with stronger initiative than 80% of peers; highly hands-on and practical
  • Enjoy helping and supporting team members
  • Strong curiosity to explore new technologies and experiment with new products, coupled with a willingness to share insights
  • Comfortable expressing genuine ideas openly and avoids overthinking

About Vattention

Vattention is a company dedicated to the transformative power of Human-AI Co-creation, believing it to be fundamental to the future of content creation. We build intelligent productivity tools that empower creators to bring their visions to life with unparalleled efficiency and impact.

Our organization is highly flat, and our team is small and talent dense. We especially appreciate people who seek truth, are passionate, and possess creativity. We thrive on intense debates, wild ideas, and shipping code.

Our team brings together talent from leading tech companies and top universities, including Alibaba, ByteDance, Tencent, Baidu, Xiaomi, Peking University, Univ. of Sci & Tech of China, Columbia University, Univ. of California Los Angeles, and Imperial College London . We also have members from AI unicorns such as Dreamina AI, Microsoft Xiaoice, Mobvoi, and 01.AI , as well as from content-driven companies and institutions like CapCut, Kwai, Joyy, Beijing Film Academy, Wondershare Filmora, and Bilibili .
