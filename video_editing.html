Video Editing

Job Description

Video editing role focused on turning briefs, scripts, references, and raw footage into publishable video deliverables.

Responsibilities:

  • Quickly understand video requirements (brief/script/references) and efficiently edit raw footage into publishable video deliverables
  • Take charge of video post-production, including editing, pacing, basic effects, subtitles and music
  • Produce horizontal (e.g. YouTube) and vertical (short video platforms) content based on platform requirements, while controlling overall rhythm and viewing experience to improve content completeness and watchability
  • Collaborate with content/operations teams to iterate and refine videos, ensuring on-time delivery

Qualifications:

  • Solid video editing foundation and ability to independently complete the full post-production workflow
  • Proficient in at least one of Premiere, Final Cut Pro, DaVinci Resolve or Avid Media Composer, and able to use CapCut (Jianying) desktop app for basic editing and export
  • Good sense of pacing, shot transitions and subtitle presentation, with solid visual taste
  • Responsible, execution-driven, with strong awareness of deadlines and delivery quality
  • Comfortable with a compensation structure where part of income is tied to completed workload

Nice to Have:

  • Experience editing for YouTube / TikTok / Shorts or similar platforms
  • Familiar with or have used AI image/video tools to improve production efficiency
  • Familiar with overseas content styles and have observation/understanding of Western social media videos

Additional Info:

  • Compensation: base salary + performance-based income tied to workload (please include relevant video work samples in your application)
