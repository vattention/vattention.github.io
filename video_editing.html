Video Editing

Job Description

视频后期制作负责创建高质量的产品演示视频、教程内容和营销素材。 您将运用创意和技术技能，将复杂的技术概念转化为引人入胜的视觉内容。

主要职责包括...

  • 制作产品演示和功能介绍视频
  • 创建技术教程和用户指南视频
  • 设计和制作营销推广视频内容
  • 处理视频后期制作，包括剪辑、调色、特效等
  • 与营销团队合作，确保内容符合品牌标准

About Vattention

Vattention is a company dedicated to the transformative power of Human-AI Co-creation, believing it to be fundamental to the future of content creation. We build intelligent productivity tools that empower creators to bring their visions to life with unparalleled efficiency and impact.

Our organization is highly flat, and our team is small and talent dense. We especially appreciate people who seek truth, are passionate, and possess creativity. We thrive on intense debates, wild ideas, and shipping code.

Our team brings together talent from leading tech companies and top universities, including Alibaba, ByteDance, Tencent, Baidu, Xiaomi, Peking University, Univ. of Sci & Tech of China, Univ. of California Los Angeles, and Sun Yat-sen University . We also have members from AI unicorns such as 01.AI , as well as from content-driven companies and institutions like CapCut, TikTok, Kwai, Joyy, Beijing Film Academy, Wondershare Filmora, Musical.ly .
