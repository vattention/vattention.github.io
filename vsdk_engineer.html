Software Engineer, Multimedia Development

Job Description

多媒体开发及算法工程师（视频编辑工具方向）负责视频编辑工具核心音视频编解码模块开发与优化，基于H.264/H.265/H.266/AV1等标准，保障编解码过程的稳定性与高效性。

Key Responsibilities:

  • Develop and optimize core audio-video codec modules for video editing tools, based on H.264/H.265/H.266/AV1 standards, ensuring stability and efficiency
  • Lead full lifecycle development of audio-video SDK, focusing on customized features for video editing scenarios, continuously optimizing SDK performance and user experience
  • Handle multi-platform adaptation and optimization for video editing tools (Windows, Mac priority, iOS, Android planned), solving cross-platform compatibility issues
  • Conduct in-depth research on audio-video codec optimization technologies, tackling technical challenges in bitrate control, quality enhancement, and algorithm acceleration
  • Develop core business modules for video editing tools, covering audio-video editing, image processing, and effects rendering
  • Track cutting-edge audio-video and multimedia technologies, exploring technical innovation in video editing user scenarios

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree or above in Computer Science, Communications, Mathematics or related fields, with 3+ years of audio-video development experience
  • Proficient in C/C++ programming, solid foundation in data structures and algorithms, skilled in multi-threading development
  • Deep understanding of audio-video codec principles, familiar with at least one encoding standard (H.264/H.265/H.266/VP8/VP9/AV1), with encoder algorithm optimization experience
  • Proficient in using FFmpeg for audio-video codec development, familiar with audio-video APIs on at least one platform (Windows/Mac/iOS/Android)
  • Familiar with computer graphics, proficient in at least one rendering framework (OpenGL, Metal)
  • Strong technical vision and self-driven ability, strong sense of responsibility and continuous learning willingness, able to independently solve complex technical problems
  • Excellent cross-team collaboration and communication skills, able to work efficiently with product and design teams to advance project implementation
  • Experience in audio-video editing SDK development, computer vision, or machine learning applications in video processing is preferred
