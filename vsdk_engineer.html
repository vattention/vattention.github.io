Software Engineer, Infrastructure

Job Description

基础设施工程师构建支持我们产品功能的平台。有时这意味着发明高可用的分布式系统。 有时这意味着处理扩展到数百万程序员的挑战。

示例项目包括...

  • 创建一个处理超过100亿文件的检索系统
  • 通过分析复杂的性能图表来优化我们的重排序库
  • 使用多种数据库、代理、缓存、任务队列和编排系统
  • 为安全计算导入图和影子检查提供基础设施

About Vattention

Vattention, is a company dedicated to the transformative power of Human-AI Co-creation, believing it to be fundamental to the future of content creation. We build intelligent productivity tools that empower creators to bring their visions to life with unparalleled efficiency and impact.

Our organization is highly flat, and our team is small and talent dense. We especially appreciate people who seek truth, are passionate, and possess creativity. We thrive on intense debates, wild ideas, and shipping code.

Our team brings together talent from leading tech companies and top universities, including Alibaba, ByteDance, Tencent, Baidu, Xiaomi, University of Science and Technology of China, University of California Los Angeles, and Imperial College London. We also have members from AI unicorns such as Dreamina AI, Microsoft Xiaoice, Mobvoi, and 01.AI, as well as from content-driven companies and institutions like CapCut, Kwai, Joyy, Wondershare Filmora, Bilibili, Beijing Film Academy, AltStory, and Newsbreak company.

Application Process

Upon an initial review, candidates will be invited for 2-3 online interviews. The final stage is an on-site visit, which includes a practical exercise (optional), in-depth discussions, and a meeting with the team.
