Growth Marketing

岗位描述 | Job Description

负责 Vattention 海外市场的增长策略与执行，围绕内容、社媒、SEO、红人合作和数据分析，推动 AI 视频产品从 0 到 1 建立可持续增长飞轮。
Own Vattention's overseas growth strategy and execution, building a sustainable 0-to-1 growth flywheel for AI video products across content, social, SEO, influencer collaboration, and data analysis.

主要职责 | Responsibilities:

  • Develop and execute overseas growth strategies; own the product's 0-to-1 stage overseas, exploring and validating sustainable, scalable growth models.
  • Have strong content taste, understand overseas trends and formats, be highly sensitive to traffic signals, and be able to create breakout content that communicates product value.
  • Build a full-funnel data monitoring system and use data insights to support product decision-making.

任职资格 | Qualifications:

  • Fluent in English as a working language; overseas work or study experience is preferred.
  • Experience growing an overseas product from 0-to-1 or 1-to-10 is preferred.
  • Familiar with mainstream US and European social platforms, with a growth methodology in at least one channel, including content marketing, influencers, SEO, paid ads, and more.
  • Passionate about frontier AI technology and product progress; interest in content is preferred.
  • Strong data analysis and logical thinking; proficient in using GA, Mixpanel, and similar tools to drive decisions.

You might be a fit if you ...

  • You are strongly curious about new products and willing to try them and share insights.
  • You are more self-driven than 80% of your peers and highly hands-on.
  • You are willing to give trust to teammates first, express your real thoughts openly, and avoid internal friction.
  • You feel energized by helping teammates.
