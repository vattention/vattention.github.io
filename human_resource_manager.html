Human Resource Manager

Job Description

Vattention believes Human-AI Co-creation is the future of content creation; we are building an AI Agent for Prosumer video post-production. We are looking for an HRM to work alongside the CEO on talent selection, organization building, and culture—so that this small, high-density team can keep hiring the right people and scale in an AI-Native, multi-location way. The role calls for someone who understands both the business and human nature, and can turn "hire right, enable well, manage risk" into practice.

Responsibilities:

  • 协助 CEO 进行人才挖掘与团队搭建：人才招募 / 背调 / Offer 谈判 / 组织文化 / 组织发展 / 薪酬设计 / 绩效激励 / 流程优化
  • 协助 CEO 建立 AI-Native 组织、多地办公组织
  • 担任不设边界的初创科技公司日常运作管家角色
  • 发挥好外部各类人才 / 财务等供应商资源的作用
  • 具备组织风控意识：建立深度的面试评价体系。针对初创团队环境，判断候选人的心理稳定性、沟通效能与文化匹配度，识别潜在的入职风险

Qualifications:

  • 本科 211 / 985 / QS100，专业不限
  • 做事干练，绝对 J 人，事事有回响
  • 内核稳定，能够成为团队同学的能量来源
  • 有科技公司初创阶段工作经验，学习能力强
  • 具备深度洞察能力：对人性与职场心理有敏锐观察，能客观预判人才在压力下的表现
  • 加分项：兼具大厂 + 创业公司经验、热爱使用各类 AI 产品为自己提效、具备最基础的财务知识、具备海外公司架构相关经验

You might be a fit if you ...

  • 自驱力高于 80% 同龄人，动手能力强
  • 能从帮助团队伙伴的过程中获得快乐
  • 乐于表达自己的真实想法，不内耗

About Vattention

Vattention is a company dedicated to the transformative power of Human-AI Co-creation, believing it to be fundamental to the future of content creation. We build intelligent productivity tools that empower creators to bring their visions to life with unparalleled efficiency and impact.

Our organization is highly flat, and our team is small and talent dense. We especially appreciate people who seek truth, are passionate, and possess creativity. We thrive on intense debates, wild ideas, and shipping code.

Our team brings together talent from leading tech companies and top universities, including Alibaba, ByteDance, Tencent, Baidu, Xiaomi, Peking University, Univ. of Sci & Tech of China, Univ. of California Los Angeles, Sun Yat-sen University, Tsinghua University, Georgia Institute of Technology, and the University of Nottingham . We also have members from AI unicorns such as 01.AI , as well as from content-driven companies and institutions like CapCut, TikTok, Huya, Kwai, Joyy, Wondershare Filmora, Musical.ly .
